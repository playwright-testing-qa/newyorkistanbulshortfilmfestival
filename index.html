About the Festival

Two cities. One screen. Endless stories.

New York Istanbul Short Film Festival celebrates independent short films from around the world, creating a cultural bridge between New York and Istanbul.


We welcome filmmakers with original voices, emotional stories, and strong cinematic vision. Our mission is to connect artists, audiences, and cultures through the power of short film.

Festival Spirit

A platform for independent voices.

Built for filmmakers who believe short films can carry powerful emotions, urgent ideas, and unforgettable moments.
